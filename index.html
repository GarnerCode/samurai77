Cyberpunk 2077, developed by CD Projekt Red and based on the tabletop RPG Cyberpunk 2020 created by Mike Pondsmith, is a sci-fi first-person shooter RPG set in the dark futuristic metropolis of Night City. First announced in May 2012 with the first trailer released in January 2013, years went by with no news on the development of the game, until E3 2018 when hype for the game was reignited with a brand new trailer, followed by E3 2019 when the official release date for the game was announced for April 15th, 2020.
