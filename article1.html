A new feature anounced by CDPR is the "Scene System", a mechanic that takes place during cutscenes, allowing players to take control of the camera and their player while the cutscene takes place. Though the level of freedom depends on the cutscene taking place, CDPR implemented in order to maximize player immersion, allowing players full control of their character during cutscenes. This feature is part of the reason CDPR decided on a controversial first-person-only perspective throughout the game, despite backlash from fans and press, though CDPR's major focus for Cyberpunk 2077 is maximum immersion, allowing players to place themselves inside V instead of simply control V from a third-person-perspective.
